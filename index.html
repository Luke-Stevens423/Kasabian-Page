MEET THE BAND


Kasabian are an English rock band formed in Leicester in 1997. They currently consist of 4 members, Tom Meighan, Sergio Pizzorno, Chris Edwards and Ian Matthews. The band have released 6 studio albums, and won several awards, including 'Best Act in the World Today' in the years 2010 and 2014.
